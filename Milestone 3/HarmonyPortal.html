Neo BETA

Neo

Neo is our very own voice assistant, designed and implemented specifically to match the needs of our valued clients who may have any kind of visual impairment.

While on this portal, you can communicate with Neo to pay your bills, redeem your credit card points, apply for a loan, or use any other feature that is available on our system, simply using the power of your voice!

When you order Neo to run any command, like paying a bill, the whole payment process will be done in the background by Neo, so you don't need to leave the portal or scroll to another page!

Neo is still in BETA and is under continual development. Help us improve Neo by reporting any issues you encounter.
